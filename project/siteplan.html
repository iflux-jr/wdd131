Website Planning Document

Site Name

PNW Hiking Collective

Domain Availability: pnwhikingcollective.org (available)

Reason for Choice: This name clearly identifies the geographic focus (Pacific Northwest), the activity (hiking), and the community aspect (collective). It's memorable, descriptive, and establishes the site's purpose immediately.

Site Purpose

The PNW Hiking Collective website serves as a central hub for hiking enthusiasts in the Pacific Northwest region. The site will provide:

  • Detailed trail reviews and recommendations
  • Up-to-date event calendar for group hikes and outdoor activities
  • Photo gallery showcasing member adventures and regional beauty
  • Resources for hiking safety, gear recommendations, and best practices
  • Community connection through membership information and contact forms

The site aims to foster community engagement, promote outdoor recreation, and provide valuable information for both novice and experienced hikers.

Target Audience Scenarios

Scenario 1: The New Hiker

Visitor: Sarah, 28, recently moved to Seattle and wants to start hiking but doesn't know where to begin.

Questions:

  • "What are some easy beginner-friendly trails near me?"
  • "What essential gear do I need for a day hike in the PNW?"
  • "Are there any group hikes where I can meet other beginner hikers?"

Scenario 2: The Experienced Hiker

Visitor: Mark, 42, seasoned hiker looking for new challenges and community involvement.

Questions:

  • "What are the most challenging backcountry trails in the North Cascades?"
  • "How can I get involved in trail maintenance or conservation efforts?"
  • "Are there any advanced hiking workshops or skills clinics coming up?"
